About

The world is full of business activities, there is a place for literally everything you may want to buy or experience in your life.

But just because a business does specialize in something, it doesn't mean that there is not a pool of common services that most of them offer.

Say, for example, that you're at a restaurant and want to go to the restroom: of course they have one! Or, again, hospitals are built to treat people, but they always have large parking lots nearby.

Then, why not make the bathroom of the restaurant accessible to everyone, even if they're not clients, for a small fee? Why not reserving a part of the hospital's parking lots for external access?

This could allow businesses to exploit their full potential and to offer services they didn't even know they had, and could create for users a vast and widespread network to rely on when travelling!
